Decentralized Storage

ICP-ChainVault integrates with leading decentralized storage blockchains to ensure the long-term integrity and security of encrypted and segmented data. This secure storage solution allows users to manage their CK BTC collateral confidently, knowing their data remains safe and intact.

Liquidity Without Selling

ICP-ChainVault enables users to mint cine USD stablecoins using CK BTC as collateral. This feature provides liquidity without the need to sell their CK BTC holdings, allowing users to leverage their assets while maintaining their investment positions.

Vault Management

ICP-ChainVault offers comprehensive vault management features. Users can create vaults to borrow, mint, or repay debt, and manage their collateral within these user-specific vaults. This streamlined process ensures efficient and secure financial management within the ICP-ChainVault platform.
